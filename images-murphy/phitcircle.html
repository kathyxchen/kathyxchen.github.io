Mission: Energize the digital health innovation ecosystem in Philadelphia

WHO WE ARE

Philadelphia Health IT Circle (PHITC) is a networking, education, and advocacy group for the Philly digital health scene. Our members include entrepreneurs, investors, health care providers, payers, patient-advocacy concerns, and academics who span the spectrum of developers and consumers of digital health products and services.

WHAT WE DO

We bring together a diverse group of stakeholders for quarterly gatherings around hot topics in health care and how technology can be best utilized to overcome barriers to innovation.

Through these meetings, PHITC provides a neutral venue for decision-makers to meet up and discuss new partnerships and ideas. By providing connections between providers, VCs, payers, industry, and Philly's robust start-up community, PHITC is a driving force for developing a fertile environment for digital health innovation in the Philadelphia region.

Are you an industrial or academic professional, a patient, payor, investor, clinician or health care provider, or working in the health system? Are you an innovator, an executive of a startup, a thought leader, or policy expert?
​
By joining us, you will be invited to quarterly meetings in the Philadelphia area, in which educational programs and important networking may be beneficial to the strengthening of the Philadelphia health system structure.
