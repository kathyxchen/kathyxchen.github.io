Unit 1: The Norms of Classical Narrative Cinema


Intro shot from Casablanca (RR111).

The property of movies to absorb us in a story is much discussed by critics and casual filmgoers alike. Sometimes described as dreamlike, other times as realistic, this type of absorbing illusion, where we feel transported into another world, is not an obvious or necessary effect of motion picture production or screening.
